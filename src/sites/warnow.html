WARNOW
Film props showcase example

Cinema & TV // Production design // Prop making // Film graphics

WARNOW, a fictional beer brand, is just a teeny tiny film prop that appears in a handful of movies that you probably haven't seen – however, out of all the many film props I've made, this is maybe the most exemplary artifact of my ventures in art direction / prop making for cinema & TV productions.
  • WARNOW can label
  • WARNOW bottle labels
  • Product shot
  • Print ads for column display (very typical for Berlin!)
Warnow on the big screen
On with the show! Although Warnow was originally produced for "Wir sind jung, wir sind stark", the cans and bottles have made it into various other larger productions:
